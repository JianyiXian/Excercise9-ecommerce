About Us

Zooby Promotional was founded by Brett Kershaw in 2006 as a promotional products supplier and custom importer. Through word of mouth we quickly grew to earn such national clients as PETCO, Microsoft, Lincoln Property Company, Fairmont Raffles Hotels International and Comerica. Zooby Promotional has worked with clients in 40 US States and three countries. While always striving to service our national customers, we've never lost the desire to help our local community. Zooby Promotional has helped raise funds for charities, schools and Universities. These Include: Lakewood Elementary, Episcopal School of Dallas, TCU and KERA. We also help small local businesses achieve their goals, such as Barley House, Kinetrol, Terilli's and CIC Coatings. Brett Kershaw received his Bachelor of Science degree in 1990 from SMU in Dallas, TX, with a Major in Economics/Finance and a Minor in Business. He is a football Letterman and was featured in ESPN's 30 for 30 series "Pony Excess"(Directed by Thaddues D. Matula). Mr. Kershaw has also appeared on local sports shows, including 1310 AM, "The Ticket". He currently lives in Dallas, TX.
